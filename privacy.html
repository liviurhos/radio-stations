Privacy Policy for Radio Live

Last updated: February 07, 2026

1. Introduction

Welcome to Radio Live ("we," "our," or "us"). We are committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how our mobile application ("App") handles information.

2. Information We Collect

Radio Live is designed with privacy in mind. We collect minimal information:

  • Favorite Stations: Stored locally on your device only. Never transmitted to our servers.
  • Station Order Preferences: Stored locally on your device only.
  • App Settings: Stored locally on your device only.

We do NOT collect:

  • Personal information (name, email, phone number)
  • Location data
  • Usage analytics or tracking data
  • Advertising identifiers

3. How We Use Information

All data stored by the App remains on your device and is used solely to:

  • Remember your favorite radio stations
  • Maintain your preferred station order
  • Save your app settings and preferences

4. Third-Party Services

Radio Live streams audio content from third-party radio stations. When you play a station:

  • Your device connects directly to the radio station's streaming server
  • The radio station may collect standard server log information (IP address, connection time)
  • We do not control the privacy practices of these radio stations
  • We recommend reviewing each station's privacy policy

Station data (names, URLs, logos) is retrieved from our content delivery network (Cloudflare Pages), which may log standard server information. We do not use this data for tracking or analytics.

5. Data Storage and Security

All your preferences and favorites are stored locally on your device using Android's SharedPreferences. This data:

  • Is never transmitted to our servers or any third party
  • Remains on your device until you uninstall the app
  • Is protected by your device's security measures

6. Children's Privacy

Radio Live does not knowingly collect any personal information from children under 13 years of age. The app is designed to be safe for all ages.

7. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by:

  • Posting the new Privacy Policy on this page
  • Updating the "Last updated" date

You are advised to review this Privacy Policy periodically for any changes.

8. Your Rights (GDPR)

If you are in the European Union, you have the right to:

  • Access: Since all data is stored locally on your device, you have full access to it
  • Deletion: You can delete all app data by uninstalling the app or clearing app data in your device settings
  • Portability: Your data is stored in a standard format on your device

9. California Privacy Rights (CCPA)

Radio Live does not sell personal information. Since we don't collect personal information, CCPA disclosure requirements do not apply.
